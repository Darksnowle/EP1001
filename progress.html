Ideation

For this project i will be working alongside Danish. Our idea is to make a hand and conveyor belt system in which he will work on the robotic arm and i will work on the conveyor belt. The idea is based off a project done by Robotics Learner where he had a system which senses the object logo and prints out what logo is on it. Since the knowledge is currently way out of my scope and also since we do not have alot of time, I discussed with Danish and come to an agreement where we just have to sense an object on the conveyor belt, make it stop for a bit and continue again.
